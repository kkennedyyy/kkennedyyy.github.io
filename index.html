Problem statement: Ramp Accessibility

Students with bikes or scooters have trouble finding ramps and parking bars.

Affinity diagram: Ramp Accessibility

In order for the problem to be solved, we'll have to track the user to know their location to see if the building is handicap accessible or provides bike bars. The app should be easy to navigate and inform users who are handicapped of their rights in regards to public buildings. We also discussed how businesses will solve this problem, how long/costly construction may be, and different ways to protect users and their privacy.

Team Personas: Ramp Accessibility

Sarah James is a college student who relies on her bike and scooter to commute to campus every day. She prefers this mode of transportation as it helps her save money, stay active, and reduce her carbon footprint.
